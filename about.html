Our Story

Precision Engineering for Industrial Excellence

Vijaya Lakshmi Enterprises has been at the forefront of manufacturing high-quality industrial process instruments, serving diverse industries across India and beyond.

Founded in 2009, we have grown from a small workshop to a leading manufacturer of temperature transmitters, pressure transmitters, RTD sensors, thermocouples, and differential pressure instruments. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for industries requiring precision measurement solutions.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with advanced machinery and testing equipment to ensure every product meets international quality standards. We pride ourselves on our ability to provide both standard products and custom solutions tailored to specific industrial requirements.
